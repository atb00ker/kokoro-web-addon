Advanced settings

When using high playback speeds, the very first word of your text may sometimes get partially or completely cut off due to audio clipping. To avoid losing the beginning of your important content, you can add a throwaway word here—this word will be spoken first, absorbing any clipping, so that your actual text is heard clearly from the start. For example, if you enter work, the system will say "work" before your actual text begins. If the first word is still clipped, try using a longer word like "elephant" as the prefix.
